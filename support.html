Notch Support

Notch is a native iPhone paper checklist app.

Basic Use

  • Tap an empty ruled line to add a task.
  • Complete a task by swiping across the row or tapping the checkbox.
  • Tap a completed checkbox again to remove the check and strike-through.
  • Long-press a task to delete it.
  • Use the bottom controls to switch, add, or delete checklist sheets.

Free And Plus

  • Free users can use up to 3 sheets.
  • Trying to add a fourth sheet opens the Notch Plus screen.
  • Notch Plus allows up to 20 sheets and removes the in-app Plus upsell popup.
  • The Plus popup appears only after meaningful checklist use and is delayed so it does not cover the immediate completion reward moment.
  • Purchases and restores are handled by Apple's App Store purchase system.
  • Restore purchases from the Plus paywall or settings sheet by tapping 購入を復元.

Data And Privacy

Notch stores checklist data locally on the device.

The current build does not require a login, account, external sync, notifications, contacts, location, camera, or microphone access.

The current build does not include third-party advertising or analytics SDKs.

Privacy policy: https://relictakumitakahashi.github.io/notch-pages/privacy.html

Troubleshooting

I cannot add a fourth sheet

Free users can create up to 3 sheets. Use Notch Plus to add up to 20 sheets.

I purchased Plus but it is not active

Open the Plus paywall or settings sheet and tap 購入を復元. Make sure the device is signed in to the Apple ID used for the purchase.

Haptics do not feel strong

Check the iPhone system haptic settings. Haptic intensity can vary by device and system settings.

I want to remove a task

Long-press the task row and confirm deletion.
