Notch Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-06-28

Overview

Notch is a native iPhone checklist app. It is designed for local, personal task lists.

The current build does not require an account, login, external sync, notifications, contacts, location, camera, or microphone access.

Data Stored On Device

Notch stores checklist data locally on the device, including:

  • Sheet names
  • Checklist item text
  • Completion state
  • App settings such as paper tone, haptic preference, plan state, Plus entitlement source, and ad cooldown timestamp

This data is stored through the app's local storage and is used only to run the checklist experience on the device.

Data We Do Not Collect

The current build does not collect or transmit:

  • Account information
  • Login credentials
  • Contact information
  • Location
  • Photos, camera, or microphone data
  • Calendar or reminder data
  • Health or financial data
  • User-generated checklist content to an external server

In-App Purchases

Notch Plus purchases and restores are handled by Apple's App Store purchase system.

Notch uses App Store purchase or restore state only to unlock Plus features in the app. Payment processing, receipts, refunds, and billing account details are handled by Apple under Apple's terms and privacy practices.

Advertising And Tracking

The current build does not include a third-party advertising SDK.

The in-app ad-like surface is a first-party Notch Plus upsell popup shown inside the app after meaningful checklist use. It is delayed so it does not cover the immediate completion reward moment, and it is not a third-party ad network placement.

The current build does not include third-party analytics SDKs and does not request App Tracking Transparency permission.

Data Sharing

The current build does not share checklist content or app usage data with third parties.

Apple may process purchase-related information when a user buys or restores Notch Plus through the App Store.

Children

Notch is not designed to knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes

This policy should be updated before release if Notch adds accounts, cloud sync, analytics, third-party advertising, notifications, contacts, location, camera, microphone, or any other data collection.
